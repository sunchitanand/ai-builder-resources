# Claude Cowork — The Full Breakdown

Based on [Anthropic's announcement](https://x.com/claudeai/status/2074525815820169320) and the [official blog](https://claude.com/blog/cowork-web-mobile/) — July 7, 2026.

---

## What Changed

Claude Cowork was desktop-only since January 2026. Now it runs on:

- **Mobile** (iOS + Android) — dispatch tasks, review results, approve decisions from your phone
- **Web** (claude.ai) — no desktop app required
- **Cloud sessions** — run on Anthropic's servers with your computer completely off

The key shift: your AI agent no longer dies when you close your laptop.

---

## The 90% Stat

Most people assume AI agents = coding tools. The data says otherwise:

- **90%+ of Cowork usage is NOT software development**
- The two biggest categories: **business operations** + **content creation** (~50% combined)
- Example tasks people actually run:
  - Reconciling quarterly spend
  - Drafting variance memos
  - Building client decks from call transcripts
  - Renewals trackers and prep docs

---

## How Cloud Sessions Work

1. **Schedule a task** — "Run my QBR prep at Monday 6 AM"
2. **Claude runs unattended** — no device online, runs on Anthropic's servers
3. **Decision reaches your phone** — when Claude hits something only you can decide, the question appears as a mobile notification
4. **You review and redirect** — approve, edit, or redirect from your phone mid-meeting
5. **Nothing ships without approval** — Claude waits for your explicit go-ahead

---

## Cloud Sessions vs Dispatch

| | Dispatch (March 2026) | Cloud Sessions (July 2026) |
|---|---|---|
| **Requires** | Desktop awake + app open | Nothing — runs serverside |
| **Your computer** | Must stay on | Can be completely off |
| **Use case** | Quick phone→desktop commands | Scheduled overnight/weekend work |
| **Availability** | All paid plans | Rolling out (Max first) |

---

## The Device Handoff

Start deep work on your desktop → close the lid → pick up exactly where it left off from your phone.

The context persists across devices. You're not starting over — you're continuing the same session.

---

## Setup

1. **Desktop** — Claude Desktop app (deep work, local files, browser control)
2. **Mobile** — Open Cowork from the sidebar of the Claude iOS/Android app
3. **Web** — claude.ai (no install needed)
4. **Cloud sessions** — schedule tasks from any device, runs on Anthropic servers

## Key Insight

It's not a chatbot. It's a persistent teammate that:
- Works when you sleep
- Follows you across devices
- Asks only when it genuinely needs your input
- Never ships without your approval

The winning workflow: set up complex tasks at your desk, let Claude run overnight, review results from your phone in the morning.
